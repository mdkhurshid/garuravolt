Compliance & Certification

Certified for the Real World

A structured certification roadmap aligned with US regulatory requirements for transport, safety, and aviation integration.

✓

Transport Safety

UN 38.3

Mandatory UN standard for lithium battery transport via air, sea, and ground. Covers altitude simulation, thermal cycling, shock, vibration, overcharge, and short circuit testing.

⬡

EV Battery Safety

UL 2580

UL standard for batteries in electric vehicles. Covers abuse tolerance, environmental exposure, and electrical safety for packs in commercial and industrial applications.

⊡

Cell & Module Safety

IEC 62133

International standard for portable sealed secondary cells. Covers mechanical, electrical, and environmental safety at the cell and module level.

✈

Aviation Battery Standard

DO-311A

RTCA standard for rechargeable lithium batteries in aircraft applications. Required pathway for FAA type certification of battery systems in manned and unmanned aircraft.

⚡

Electrical Safety

UL 1973

Standard for batteries in stationary, vehicle auxiliary, and light electric rail applications. Covers cell-level and pack-level abuse and performance testing.

🔋

Supply Chain Compliance

NDAA Compliant

Cell sourcing with full supply chain transparency. NDAA and tariff-risk flagged vendor matrix for government contract eligibility.
